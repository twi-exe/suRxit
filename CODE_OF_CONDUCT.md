# Code of Conduct
All contributors are expected to uphold a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment. Discrimination or abusive behavior will not be tolerated. Please report any violations to the project maintainers.

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Enforcement
- Reports will be reviewed confidentially## Our Standards
- Consequences may include removal from the project- Be respectful and considerate
- No harassment, bullying, or discrimination
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
